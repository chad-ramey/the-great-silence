The Great Silence

Every application tracked. Every outcome recorded. Understanding the patterns is how you stop taking the silence personally.

29 applications tracked Updated June 7, 2026 Technology and operations roles

Most of the outcomes here were not decisions about a person. Five roles closed before any evaluation took place. Several more were filled by candidates already in process before this search began. That is market timing and volatility, not a skills assessment.

Two recruiters — Tracey Kim at Netflix and Dan Spencer at Life360 — wrote something specific and human. What they said, how briefly they said it, and what they offered next is the benchmark the rest of the data is measured against. It cost them maybe two minutes.

Tracking this removes the guesswork. When you can see the patterns clearly, you can stop waiting on the silence and start building your own signal. The search continues.
